What's next?

I always want a new bike. Here's a few that have caught my eye.

Honda

Being real, I'm a Honda guy. I've had several, and though I think many of their bikes are hideous, I like a lot of them.

Honda Fury 1300

I originally thought that the Fury was ugly and a bad attempt of breaking into an industry sector that was dying out anyway. The success of Discovery Channel shows about bike builders brought on this atrocity, but as I get older I see the Fury as a reliable, cheap way to get a tough looking bike, with the help of a hacksaw of course.

Honda CB1100

You've seen my '71 CB500, so you know I like that old flat, Universal Japanese Motorcycle look. I was stoked when they created the new CB1100 with bits of that old style yet with an updated motor. Now if only I can ride one without the hip pain that by old CB gave me.

Honda Rebel 500

I never thought I'd be interested in a Rebel, but these new ones look fun.

Harley

I'll need to get a garage first.

Softail Springer

A long Softail with a springer front. It doesn't need any chrome because I'm not going to keep it clean anyway.

Dyna Wide Glide

This is my backup bike if I can't afford the Softail. Actually, I'd try to get a Softail with Wide Glide front forks if I can't get the Springer, then next would be the Dyna.
